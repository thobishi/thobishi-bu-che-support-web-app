What does it cost?

Fees for the accreditation of private providers and each of their programmes, as well as site visits, where applicable, are indicated below. These fees cover the administrative costs to the HEQC in processing applications, including payments to independent evaluators.

Accreditation Fees for Private Higher Education Institutions as of 1 April 2016

Item Revised fees
Accreditation Phase / Re-accreditation Programme Application Fees R 11 000.00
Additional sites of delivery per programme R 2 500.00
Once-off Fee for accreditation of new providers R 9 000.00
Site visit Fee R 6 500.00
Additional sites of delivery per site visit R 2 500.00
Additional direct costs of travel, accommodation, evaluator fees and subsistence for evaluators will be charged on top of these fees.
Representation / Deferral (additional information) / New or Additional Site of Delivery R 3 850.00
Deferral (Additional evaluation) R 0.00
Conditions R 550.00
