What does it cost?

The Programme Accreditation Directorate fees for the processes linked to accreditation and re-accreditation of private providers and each of their programmes, as well as site visits, where applicable, are indicated below. These fees cover the administrative costs to the HEQC in processing applications, including payments to independent evaluators. In addition, a fee is levied on any provider relocating sites and/or extending accredited programmes to sites of delivery without approval by the CHE.
